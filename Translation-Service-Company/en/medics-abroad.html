Approbation of medics in EU countries

Our agency provides document packages arrangement services required for approbation of medics across the European Union member-states, including:

1.

Authentication, translation and legalization of diploma, supplement to diploma, academic transcript, certificates, etc.

2.

Obtaining a Certificate of Vocational Qualification and Specialty Certificate.

3.

Registration at the Regional Body of the Bulgarian Medical Association, Bulgarian Dental Association, Bulgarian Association of Healthcare Professionals, etc.

4.

Obtaining and certification of syllabus, academic transcripts, information, certificates, forms, etc.

5.

Obtaining a Certificate of Good Medical Practice.

6.

Authentication at the consular offices of foreign diplomatic missions /if necessary/.

7.

Support in issuing documents by universities, colleges, government departments or organizations.

8.

Obtaining a Certificate of Good Conduct.

9.

Obtaining a Certificate of Higher Educational Institution Accreditation issuing diplomas pertaining thereto.

10.

Delivery of document by a dispatch courier service on client’s demand to a given address.

The abovementioned services are rendered by a Letter of Authorization on submission of the required documents.
