Our agency provides support for issuing a single entry visa and multiple-entry visa related to tourist, business, private, conference or any other trips and tours.

  • Individual advice on visa requirements for many countries
  • Collection, completion and obtaining of the required travel documents
  • Submission of documents to diplomatic missions within or outside the Republic of Bulgaria
  • Obtaining passport with visa affixed thereto
  • Delivery of document by a dispatch courier service /on demand/

All countries, requiring entry visa and frequently visited by the Bulgarian nationals, are set out herewith: China, Russia, Kazakhstan, United States of America (USA), Republic of South Africa (RSA), Azerbaijan, Australia, Canada, India, Nigeria and etc.
