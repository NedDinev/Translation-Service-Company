Why health insurance is necessary?

As all life and health-associated risks are beyond individual control and might befall in emergency and disaster situations, they pose a serious challenge to us, in particular while outside the Republic of Bulgaria.

If you are travelling abroad, we strongly recommend that you get a medical insurance for a period of time and coverage according to each country standards to prevent troubles when on a vacation, business trip, training or business.

Health insurance coverage

Health insurance for traveling abroad with Assistance ensures Your safety and provides protection in the event of an accident or an unexpected (acute) disease 24/7 days a week and covers all costs related to a timely free specialized medical aid at the nearest or the desired medical institution. Medical services costs are covered directly by the Insurer within the insurance limits.

Health insurance validity

Health insurance is valid for 1 to 365 days for Europe, worldwide to the exclusion of the USA and Canada and worldwide with the USA and Canada coverage included.

What do we offer?

  • Accidence insurance policy with Assistance medical and transportation costs at home and abroad
  • Risk insurance with Assistance medical and transportation costs for multiple trips - multitrip
  • Travel insurance, accident insurance
  • Statutory health insurance for foreign nationals residing in Bulgaria
  • Statutory health insurance for foreign nationals staying in or traveling across Bulgaria

We offer various types of health insurance in terms of sums insured or general terms and conditions.

We provide professional advisory services and an opportunity to opt for health insurance that is deemed most appropriate in the circumstances.
