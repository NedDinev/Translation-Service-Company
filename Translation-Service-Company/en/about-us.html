Timag - translation and travel agency is an assignee of a company, registered in 1992, ranking amongst pioneer travel companies in the tourism industry.

The scope of business includes a wide range of services catering to the needs of transport and hotel accommodation services, visa and advisory services, documents translation and legalization, health insurance, etc.

We are granted a certificate of registration of a tour operator and travel agency under number 492 and awarded a contract for translation of documents and other papers from the Bulgarian language into foreign languages and vice versa by the Bulgarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

We have a proven track record over the years in providing professional services.
